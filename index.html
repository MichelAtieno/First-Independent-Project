ABOUT ME

My name is Michel Atieno. I am currently a student at Moringa School.
Two years ago, i had an interest in taking on entrepreneurship full time and I started working as a personal assistant for a small business owner. There was a computer and I had been self-teaching in html and css (months prior) but I did not have all the tools i needed to be successful. I was pretty good at running the business and most of the time, the owner left me alone to run things by myself. I gained a lot of skills in terms of being social, handling different types of situations sometimes hostile,keeping time and keeping accounts up-to-date. As much as I loved the job, In February 2017,I had reached a ceiling in terms of learning new things and I wanted a career. I resigned from the position and on the same day, I went and signed up for a class called Cisco Certified Networking Associate (CCNA). I knew nothing about it but after a few weeks into it, I was completely immersed. I spent so many hours on it without getting tired or frustrated because I was solving problems and making devices work, linking them, securing them etc.
I was also very curious about opportunities peers studying Computer Science were getting. After completing my CCNA certificate, I wanted to delve deeper into tech and that is how i ended up at Moringa School .
The Prep Course at Moringa School has helped me discover more about myself and has most definitely elevated my skill level in creativity, critical thinking, problem-solving and most importantly soft skills required in a professional work environment. I must also say i have grown personally and I am more confident in undertaking tasks. The key has been more research, excessive practice and proper time-management. I hope to continue with the same spirit as i continue my software development journey and to be prepared for more challenges that I hope to solve.
